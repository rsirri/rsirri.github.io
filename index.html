About Me

I'm an SAP Technical Architect based in Alpharetta, GA with 14+ years of experience across large-scale S/4HANA implementations, migrations, upgrades, and support engagements.

Currently the retained lead technical resource at Savancys on a large-scale S/4HANA engagement for one of North America's largest construction and engineering firms — brought through an employer transition by the client.
